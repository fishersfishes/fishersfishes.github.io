About Us

Welcome to Fisher's Fishes, a journal club focused on theoretical biology. We're a group of people from research institutes all over India who are interested in the interdisciplinary field of theoretical biology involving but not limited to quantitative/computational biology, mathematical biology, and the physics of life. We meet regularly to discuss fascinating papers and research. The intended audience is undergraduate through PhD students.
