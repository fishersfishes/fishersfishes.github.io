About Us

Welcome to Fisher's Fishes, a support group for Theoreticians interested in Biology, this club started off as a theoretical biology journal club at IISER Pune but due to lack of critical mass in once place, we expanded virtually to include people from various institutes primarily having undergraduate and graduate students, we've had regular talks by students about fascinating papers or their own research work. Right now we're looking to expand into a magazine and are actively looking for article contributions loosely based around the theme "Theoretical Biology". Contact Abhishek (contact information given below) if you'd like to be added to our mailing list. The links for the whatsapp group and the discord server are at the bottom of the page

The details including joining links about talks are first updated on our whatsapp group
